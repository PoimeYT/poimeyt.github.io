NRL TIPPING

The Comp Rules

— The Basics —
Platform
01 All tipping is done through the NRL Tipping website. The link is posted in the group chat. Everyone must join the comp using the link.
02 Tips lock automatically when each game kicks off — no extensions, no exceptions.
03 Points are tracked by the site automatically. 1 point per correct tip.
— Tiebreakers —
Tiebreakers
01 If two or more people are tied at the end of the season, whoever had the most correct tips during the finals wins.
02 Still tied? Whoever was closest to the correct margin in the grand final wins.
03 Still tied after that? Coin flip. We're not overthinking it.
— Forfeits —
Forfeits
01 Whoever finishes bottom of the tipping ladder at the end of each round must spin the forfeit wheel.
02 The forfeit must be completed before the first game of the following round kicks off.
03 Proof of completion must be posted in the group chat.

⚠️ Didn't do your forfeit? You must send a full body photo of yourself to be made into a Viggle.ai video — chosen and created by the group. The video gets posted in the chat, no arguments.

⚠️ Viggle.ai is limited! You can only use Viggle.ai once per month per person. Make it count — no exceptions.

💡 Note: If multiple people are tied at the bottom of the ladder after a round, all tied players spin the wheel and complete a forfeit.

— General —
General
01 New players joining mid-season start from 0 points.
02 The winner gets bragging rights plus whatever prize the group agrees on before the season starts.
03 No whinging about results, refs, or your picks. You tip, you live with it.

Good luck — you'll need it 🏉
